Boo!! Didn't mean to scare you, or did we?!? Bet you didn't think we'd be back at Sunny's so soon! On Friday, October 27th we'll be kicking off the holiday weekend. We'll be haunting the back room by making our extended jams spookier than ever, and even reanimating some deceased songs that will return as zombies!

Stevie From St. Louie opens the night before we launch into three sets of reggae and bluegrass. On bass we have Derrick Bourne, on drums Yahoteh Kokayi, on banjo Doug Goldstein, Ken Shoji on fiddle and as always, Sasha'mani Francois on vocals and Mark Anselm on guitar. There's no cover at Sunny's just a mighty fine bar.

CITY BILLIES

Reggae Bluegrass

Formed in 2008 by Sasha'mani Francois and Mark Anselm, City Billies includes some of the most talented and eclectic musicians in Brooklyn. Sasha’mani and Mark grew up listening to folk, bluegrass, country and old time music and met when their reggae bands crossed paths.

They released their debut EP 2011 with more singles and a new video being released in 2017. City Billies has performed at venues such as Littlefield, Southpaw, Sunny's Bar and Rockwood Music Hall.
